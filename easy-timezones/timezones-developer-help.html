Common Questions

Does the app require an account?

Does the app collect my data?

How do I request a feature?

Send a short description to a.gideonpaul@gmail.com. Include examples or screenshots if possible.

Troubleshooting

  • Restart the app and try again.
  • Verify your device time and time zone are set correctly.
  • Make sure you are running the latest app version.
  • If a timezone looks incorrect, share the exact city or region.

What to Include in a Support Request

  • Device: iPhone model or Android device model
  • OS version: iOS or Android version
  • App version: Found in the app settings or App Store listing
  • Steps: What you did and what you expected to happen
Providing these details helps us troubleshoot faster.
